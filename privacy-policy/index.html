⏰ AlarmPal
Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective immediately on app launch

Short version: AlarmPal stores all your data locally on your device. We don't run servers, we don't sell your data, and we don't track you. Certain features send requests to third-party APIs (Gemini, weather, facts), but only with your permission.

1. What Data We Collect

AlarmPal does not collect or transmit personal data to our own servers. We have no backend. All data lives on your device.

The following data is stored locally on your device only using Android DataStore and Room database:

  • Alarm schedules (times, labels, days)
  • App settings and preferences
  • Your Gemini API key (stored in plaintext DataStore — encrypted storage is on our roadmap)
  • Wake-up briefing cache (weather, calendar summary, fun facts)
  • Accountability buddy phone number (stored locally, used only to send SMS)
  • Sleep tracking data (if enabled)

2. Sensitive Permissions We Request

AlarmPal requests the following permissions. Here's exactly why each one is needed:

  • 📷 Camera — Required for the optional "Face Game" alarm dismissal challenge (ML Kit smile/expression detection). Video never leaves your device.
  • 📍 Location — Used to automatically detect your city for local weather in the morning briefing. Coarse location only. Used once per alarm trigger, not continuously tracked.
  • 📅 Calendar — Reads your upcoming calendar events to include a "heads up for today" in your morning briefing. Read-only access. No events are transmitted.
  • 👥 Contacts — Used only to let you pick a buddy phone number from your contacts in the alarm editor. Read-only access.
  • 💬 SMS (SEND_SMS) — Sends accountability messages to your designated buddy if you miss an alarm. Messages are sent directly from your SIM card — AlarmPal sees no message logs, and messages are not routed through any server.
  • 🔔 Notifications — Required to schedule and surface alarm notifications on Android 13+.
  • ⏰ Schedule Exact Alarm — Required to fire alarms at precisely scheduled times (Android 12+).
  • 🖥️ Display Over Other Apps — Required to show the alarm screen over a locked screen on Android 10+.

3. Third-Party Services

AlarmPal may contact the following third-party services, all initiated on your device in response to a specific feature request:

  • Google Gemini API — When Cloud AI wakeup briefing enhancement is enabled and you have entered a valid API key, your device sends a text prompt (weather + calendar summary) to Google's servers. Your API key is stored locally and sent with this request. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Open-Meteo — Fetches weather forecasts. Only your city name or approximate coordinates are sent. No personal information. Subject to Open-Meteo Terms.
  • uselessfacts.jsph.pl — Fetches a random fun fact for your morning briefing. No personal data is sent. This is a public API.

4. Accountability SMS (Buddy Feature)

When you assign an accountability buddy and send them an opt-in request or missed-alarm alert, an SMS is sent directly from your device's SIM via Android's SmsManager API. AlarmPal does not see, store, or log this message content. Message delivery is handled entirely by your mobile carrier.

We send a consent ("opt-in") SMS to your buddy when you first add them, explaining the feature and how to opt out.

5. Children's Privacy

AlarmPal is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. To delete all AlarmPal data: go to Settings → Apps → AlarmPal → Clear Data on your Android device, or uninstall the app.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
